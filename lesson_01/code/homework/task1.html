Tyler Durden 1,83 American No data Dissociative anarchist

Imagine a rational ordinary person. So Tyler Durden is the complete opposite of this person.

Tyler is a minimalist and neo-luddite who is smart, capable, charismatic and intuitive.

He greatly oppose societal programming, rampant consumerism, popular culture and human society in the modern era.

Occupation

Tyler has a very diverse occupation, such as:

  • Maker and seller of soaps
  • Creator of Fight Club
  • Leader of Project Mayhem

Strengths

Tyler Durden has several strengths:

  • Advanced fighting skills
  • High endurance
  • Chemistry knowledge
  • High intelligence
  • Charisma
  • Leadership
  • Manipulation

Goals

Tyler also has main goals that he strives for:

  1. Drive society to chaos by destroying economic buildings
  2. Convince the downtrodden into joining Fight Club to become more confident by using violence
